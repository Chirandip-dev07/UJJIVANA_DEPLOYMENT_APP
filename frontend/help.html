    Getting Started with Ujjivana

    Create Account

    Sign up and set up your profile to start your environmental education journey.

    Take Tour

    Explore the platform features with our interactive guided tour.

    To create an account:

    1. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation
    2. Fill in your details (name, email, password)
    3. Select your role (Student or Teacher)
    4. Verify your email address
    5. Complete your profile setup

    Yes! Ujjivana is completely free for students and teachers. Our mission is to make environmental education accessible to everyone.

    Account Management

    If you've forgotten your password:

    1. Go to the login page
    2. Click on "Forgot Password"
    3. Enter your email address
    4. Check your email for a password reset link
    5. Follow the instructions to set a new password

    To update your profile:

    1. Click on your profile picture in the top navigation
    2. Select "My Profile" from the dropdown menu
    3. Click the "Edit Profile" button
    4. Update your information and click "Save Changes"

    Learning Features

    Learning modules are structured educational content on various environmental topics:

    • Each module contains multiple lessons
    • Complete lessons in sequence or skip around
    • Take quizzes to test your knowledge
    • Earn points and badges for completion
    • Track your progress in your dashboard

    You earn points through various activities:

    • Completing lessons: 10-50 points each
    • Passing quizzes: 20-100 points each
    • Participating in events: 50-200 points each
    • Completing challenges: 100-500 points each
    • Daily login bonus: 5 points

    Points can be redeemed for rewards in the Rewards section.

    As a teacher, you can create custom learning modules:

    1. Go to your Teacher Dashboard
    2. Click on "Create New Module"
    3. Add module title, description, and category
    4. Create lessons with text, images, and videos
    5. Add quizzes and assessments
    6. Publish the module for your students

    Community Features

    To participate in challenges:

    1. Go to the Challenges section
    2. Browse available challenges
    3. Click on a challenge to view details
    4. Click "Join Challenge" to participate
    5. Complete the challenge requirements
    6. Submit your completion for verification

    The leaderboard ranks users based on:

    • Total points earned
    • Modules completed
    • Challenges completed
    • Events attended
    • Consistent participation

    Leaderboards are updated in real-time and there are different categories (weekly, monthly, all-time).

    To register for events:

    1. Go to the Events section
    2. Browse upcoming events
    3. Click on an event to view details
    4. Click "Register Now" if registration is open
    5. Complete any required registration forms
    6. You'll receive a confirmation and event details

    Some events may have limited spots, so register early!

    Troubleshooting

    If Ujjivana is loading slowly, try these solutions:

    • Refresh the page
    • Clear your browser cache and cookies
    • Try using a different browser
    • Check your internet connection
    • Disable browser extensions that might be interfering

    If the problem persists, please contact our support team.

    If you're having trouble logging in:

    1. Make sure you're using the correct email and password
    2. Check if Caps Lock is on (passwords are case-sensitive)
    3. Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link
    4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
    5. Try logging in from a different browser or device

    If none of these work, contact our support team for assistance.

    If your progress isn't being saved:

    • Make sure you have a stable internet connection
    • Don't close the browser tab until you see a confirmation message
    • Complete lessons and quizzes in one session when possible
    • Check if you're logged in (your username should appear in the top right)
    • Try refreshing the page and checking your progress again

    If the issue continues, contact support with details about what you were trying to save.
